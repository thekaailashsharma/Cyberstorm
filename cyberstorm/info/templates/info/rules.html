Rules


General Rules

Fair play is mandatory. No cheating or exploits.

Only one account per participant.

Respect fellow participants and organizers.

Organizers' decisions are final.

Team Formation Rules

Teams must have 2-4 members.

No participant can join multiple teams.

Register before the event starts.

Challenge Rules

Topics include Web, Crypto, OSINT etc.

Points vary based on difficulty.

Submit flags in flag{example} format.



Prohibited Actions

No DDoS or server attacks.

No flag sharing between teams.

No exploiting unintended bugs.

No phishing or illegal activities.

Scoring and Prizes

Rankings based on total points.

In case of a tie, last solved challenge decides.

Winners receive prizes & certificates.
